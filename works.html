SF City Clinic - Web Redesign
San Francisco City Clinic’s
A digital transformation for an health clinic

Introduction

San Francisco City Clinic’s mission is to improve our community’s sexual health by providing free or low-cost sexual health care. The goal was to redesign their website to provide a user-centric environment that provided their information in a structured and meaningful way to achieve a stronger, trusting, and healthier community for all individuals.

My Role: UX/UI Designer

Tools: Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator, Invision

Deliverables: UI style guide, brand voice, IA, site map, wireframes, & responsive high-fidelity mockups
